About StreamTune

StreamTune is a Chrome extension designed to simplify your music creation workflow. Instantly analyze the BPM and key of any song streaming on your browser, making it easier to mix, match, and create new tracks.

Features

  • Real-time audio analysis directly from your browser tab.
  • Automatic detection of BPM and musical key.
  • User-friendly interface with instant results.
  • Support for a wide range of streaming services.
